FOURTH DIMENSIONAL: Summer of Fun

Fourth Dimensional tells the story of two friends – Ava, a young woman trying to figure her life out; and Mon, an Extra-Dimensional fugitive whose escaped to Earth. Unfortunately for both of them, Mon has brought all the dangers and oddities of his dimension with him. Together, Mon and Ava will have to face these other fugitives, and find their places in a world unwelcome to them.

Fourth Dimensional is an Urban Sci-Fi Adventure series, so if you’re a fan of adventure comics like Bone and Madman, or action manga like Dragon Ball and One Piece, you’re gonna love 4D.
